Summary: Latitude vs. X



"What's the weather like as we approach the equator?"

That is the question I hoped to answer through the WeatherPy homework assignment.

For this analysis, I pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API to assemble a dataset on over 500 cities. After assembling the dataset, I used the Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude, including temperature, cloudiness, wind speed and humidity. This website serves as my dashboard to share my findings!
