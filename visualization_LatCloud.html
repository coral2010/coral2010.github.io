Latitude vs. Cloudiness



While there is a wide range of cloudiness % over the different latitudes, there seems to be a greater concentration of cities with a cloudiness % of 0 away from the equator. Closer to the equator, at a latitude of a range of about -5 to 20, cloudiness % is higher.
